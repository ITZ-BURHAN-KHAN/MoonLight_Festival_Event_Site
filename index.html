Tours and Events FAQ

Didn’t find the answer you are looking for? Email us and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.
Please note, the following answers are based on most situations. There may be some exceptions at specific events.
Do tickets sell out?
Oh, yes. Especially the weekends. We always encourage people to buy their tickets as far in advance as possible.
Are tours cancelled if there is inclement weather?
Depends on the tour and weather. Walking tours are most vulnerable to storms and extreme cold. Driving tours are mostly cancelled if it is really bad.S
What precautions are you taking for COVID-19?
Social distancing is maintained and masks are requested, and required in driving tours. We have also reduced our maximum attendance to ensure that everyone can stay six feet apart on walking tours.
How much do tours cost?
The Ghost Walks are $35 for adults (13 and up), Children (7-12) are $20…other tours have different rates
Where do the tours meet?
We do not have a shop or physical store that tours begin at. The different tours meet at different locations and times. Details are contained in the confirmation email you receive when you make a reservation.
Do I get a discount for applying to multiple events?
Unfortunately, because we produce all events on behalf of different non-profits, we cannot offer discounts for multiple events.
